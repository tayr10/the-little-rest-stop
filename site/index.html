Our Story

The kind of diner you tell your friends about.

The Little Rest Stop is exactly what the name promises: a place to slow down, pull up a seat, and feel genuinely taken care of. Tucked off Brookfield Road in Sturbridge, we've been feeding this community with pride — and it's always worth the detour.

Every dish starts from scratch. The pulled pork is made in-house. The cheddar cheese sauce on the Signature Spuds? Homemade. The coffee? Hot, strong, and free refills on the house.
